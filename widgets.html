Widgets

Small interactive things — pharmacokinetics calculators, dynamical-systems toys, math demos. Each one is a self-contained page you can fiddle with directly in the browser. No accounts, no tracking, no server round-trips: the math runs in your tab.

Built with D3 and a little hand-rolled glue. If something does not match your intuition, that is often where the interesting modeling assumption is hiding — the controls expose the parameters I made educated guesses about.
